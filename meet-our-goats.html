Meet The Goats!

We at the Feta Company take pride in how we treat our goats. Without the goats our company would be nothing, and as such, we treat them like members of the company. We would like for you to meet some of our top goats so that you can see just how good of a life they're living while they help us produce the delicious feta cheese that we all love. Take a look!
